About Us

OUR VISION

Our vision is to provide highly focused and encouraging environment by imparting technical knowledge at early ages which enables students to realize their fullest potential and thus, preparing to make their best contributions to society

OUR MISSION

Our aim is to produce young minds that learn regular academics along with technology at an early age. We aim to create a collaboration of students, parents and the society to create foundation to participate responsibly, to utilize technology, to succeed in the post-secondary education and gain edge through the technical knowledge acquired

Razal academy success lies in sincere effort of its Directors and Trainers. The Center provides special and specific courses for the specific needs of its students. It is committed to the intellectual learning fulfillment of its students that range from individuals from young to old and for everyone. Individualized Learning Plan
Is your student a gifted learner? Does he have trouble grasping math? Because of our individualized approach to learning, our online academy takes the class down to a one-on-one level and focuses just on your learner. With your student at the center, we make lessons fit around him, instead of trying to fit him into pre-made lessons like traditional schools that have trouble addressing unique needs or abilities.
Progress Checkpoints
Our faculty ensures progress with checkpoints placed at strategic locations during your student’s learning journey. If your student struggles or has questions, teachers are available to assist by phone, email, or an online classroom. Parents are also an essential element in the learning process, so we communicate regularly with parents about student progress. Lesson plans are flexible to changes in student’s mastery of concepts or lifestyle needs. Academic integrity is also reviewed to regulate true progress. Lessons Adjustable to Lifestyle

Accreditation Assurance

Like a stamp of excellence, accreditation means your student is receiving rigorous academics and the highest quality education possible.
