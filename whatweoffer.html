What We Offer

Aurora Academy

Online learning gives you freedom. Auroras provides Whitmore School –CompuHigh and American Islamic school, which gives your child access to the best in qualified education. We strive to make a structured, yet flexible at-home learning environment where your student thrives. Our accredited online academy combines rigorous academics together with personalized learning options to give you a truly custom-fit educational solution. When you enroll at AURORA, your student receives an individualized learning plan based on his/her goals, his current academic standing, and his/her style of learning. A personalized academic support team of teachers, advisors, and counselors is then selected to ensure your student has a strong foundation for achievement.

In School Program (STEM)

Students will experience building models and also get introduced to new mechanical concepts. Catered to the students of grade 1 to grade 4
The children may also learn to modify small part of the game by writing simple code to change functionalities. Catered to the students of grade 7 to grade 8.
During this course, children will be introduced to Java programming through blocks, to modify some of the simple games. Therefore, they will be programming and testing their work on the actual game and a simulator. Basics of Microcontrollers will be taught by building simple models.
